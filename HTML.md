# HTML Cheat Sheet

## Links

### Linking text
```<a href="https://url.com">Description of link</a>```

### Linking an image
<a href="https://url.com">
  <img src="https://url.com">
</a>

## Images

### Linking to an online image
<img src="https://url.com">   [<..."url" />]?

### Linking to a local image
<img src="">
